Accreditations

Being a New Energy Tech Approved Seller means this business meets high standards for customer service, product quality, and ethical sales practices when it comes to solar, battery storage, and other energy technologies. It gives you peace of mind that you're dealing with a trusted provider who follows industry best practices and puts your needs first.

Accreditation with Solar Victoria ensures the business is eligible to offer state government rebates and incentives. Choosing an accredited provider helps protect your investment and guarantees you're getting a safe, reliable solar system.
