Mark Cierra

For Mark, being an Elvis tribute artist isn’t about pretending to be someone else — it’s about honouring a legend, sharing joy, and creating memories that last long after the final note fades.

Mark Cierra’s journey as an Elvis impersonator began long before he ever stepped onto a stage. Growing up, Mark spent countless afternoons listening to his father’s treasured Elvis records—classics like Can’t Help Falling in Love, Suspicious Minds, and Burning Love filled the house and sparked a fascination that would follow him for life.

As a teenager, Mark began mimicking Elvis’s vocals for fun, but friends and family quickly noticed something special: he didn’t just sound like Elvis… he felt like Elvis. Encouraged by their reactions, he performed at local community events, school shows, and family gatherings, slowly building the confidence and presence that now define his performances.

In his early twenties, Mark committed fully to his craft. He studied Elvis’s concerts, perfected the iconic moves, invested in handmade replica jumpsuits, and trained his voice to capture the warmth, power, and emotion that made Elvis unforgettable. Through years of dedication, he developed his own signature blend of authenticity and charisma — not an imitation, but a heartfelt tribute.

Today, Mark Cierra performs across Australia at weddings, festivals, private events, and theatres, always aiming to recreate the magic that captivated him as a child. Audiences praise him for his energy, his attention to detail, and his ability to transform a room into a moment from music history.
