About

From the moment Mark Cierra first heard Elvis on his father’s old vinyl collection, he knew music would shape his life. What started as a simple love for the King’s voice grew into a lifelong passion for bringing his spirit back to the stage. Today, Mark is known for his powerful vocals, authentic costumes, and magnetic showmanship that leave audiences saying, “It felt like Elvis was right there with us.”
